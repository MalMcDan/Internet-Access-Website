Internet Access Assistance With Hampton City Schools

Hampton City Schools is dedicated to helping our students achieve their best academically, which is why we offer help for getting internet assistance.

Families who have no Internet access at home may qualify for low or no cost Internet access through programs provided by HCS working in partnership with T-Mobile, Cox Communications and the federal eRate program. In order to qualify, the family must not currently have Internet access at home through any other means and be on some form of public assistance.

If you’re interested in getting assistance, please fill out the form. If you would like to know more about the programs we use to help with internet assistance, please click on About ACP, or About Mifis.
